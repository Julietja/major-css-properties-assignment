Difference Between Padding, Margins and Border

In CSS, a margin is the space around an element's border, while padding is the space between an element's border and the element's content.
Put another way, the margin property controls the space outside an element, and the padding property controls the space inside an element.
On the other hand, a border that goes around the padding and content.
